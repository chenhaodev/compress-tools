L1 penalized least squares with SALSA

This folder contains MATLAB functions to implement:

  • basis pursuit (BP),
  • basis pursuit denoising (BPD),
  • dual basis pursuit (dual BP),
  • dual basis pursuit denoising (dual BPD).

MATLAB functions

  • BP.m
  • BPD.m
  • dualBP.m
  • dualBPD.m

Examples

Illustrative examples with real and complex data.

  • BP with DFT (complex data)
  • BPD with DFT
  • BPD with STFT
  • dual BP with DFT and STFT
  • dual BPD with DFT and STFT
  • BPD with DFT (complex data)
  • BPD with STFT (complex data)
  • dual BPD with DFT and STFT (complex data)

The implementation here assumes that A1 and A2 are Parseval frames; i.e., A A^H = I
